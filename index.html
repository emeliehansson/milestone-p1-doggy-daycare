About us


Welcome to Doggy Daycare, where your dog can come and stay for the day when you're at work or for a couple of days when you need a vacation.


We promise a lot of long walks, playtime, new friends and maybe some treats!


We welcome dogs of all breeds and already have a variety of friends that stay with us on a daily basis.

What we offer


Full time daycare: €300/month. If your family has more than one dog in need of daycare, the next dog will recieve a discount of €100/month.

Part time daycare: €200/month. If your family has more than one dog in need of daycare, the next dog will recieve a discount of €50/month.

Extra daycare (outside of regular hours): Contact us and we'll discuss it further.
